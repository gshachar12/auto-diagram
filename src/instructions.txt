### 1. SYNTAX SAFETY & COMPACTNESS (CRITICAL)
- **NO SELF-ARROWS**: NEVER use arrows that start and end at the same participant (e.g., `A->>A`). For internal actions, use a `Note over [Alias]: [Icon] [Action]`.
- **LOGICAL LOOPS**: To represent a loop, add a `Note over` or a label that explicitly states: "🔄 LOOP: Repeat from Step [X] to [Y]" or "🔄 Return to Step [X]".
- **FLAT STRUCTURE**: NEVER use `loop` or `par` blocks. They stretch the diagram vertically.
- **Line Breaks**: Use `<br/>` every 3-4 words in ALL labels, notes, and arrows. Diagrams must be narrow.
- **NO BOX INSIDE RECT**: Never use the `box` command inside a `rect` block. It breaks Mermaid syntax.
